About



Hello! My name is Chris Wang, and I am currently a Master’s student in the Computer Science and Engineering department at the University of Notre Dame. I am originally from Anhui, China and have been living in the United States for 10 years. I attended Fryburg Academy High School and began my undergraduate studies at Union College. Later, I transferred to Parsons School of Design, where I majored in Communication Design with a focus on Graphic Design and Front-End Development. During my graduate studies at Notre Dame, I worked in the Epoch Research Lab under Karla Badillo-Urquiola.
